The Method & Risk

The Gurman Method is a structured educational methodology developed by Oscar Gurman to select assets, manage risk and study recurring premium strategies through options.

The Wheel Strategy is based on selling put and call options on quality stocks or ETFs, with the objective of collecting option premiums in a systematic way.
