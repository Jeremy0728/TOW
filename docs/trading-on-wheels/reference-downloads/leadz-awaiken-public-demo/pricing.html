Know what you are joining

view all FAQs

What exactly do I get with VIP?

VIP gives you community access, live trade alerts, weekly Q&A, strategy breakdowns, trade cards, and ongoing education around the Gurman Method.

Is this just a signals group?

No. Trade ideas can be discussed, but the priority is understanding the logic, risk, and review process behind decisions.

Do I need options experience?

Beginners can start with the course foundations, while experienced traders can use the membership to sharpen execution and risk management.

What happens after I join?

You follow onboarding, enter the private room, review the member resources, and begin studying weekly market context and trade logic.

Is this financial advice?

No. Trading On Wheels is trading education and community. We share methods, analysis and our own trades, not personalized financial advice.

Where should I start?

Join membership if you want the private room and live context. Start with the course if you need the foundation first.
