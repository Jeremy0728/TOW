How Competitions Work

Each competition is structured around rules, position sizing, trade review, and leaderboard tracking. The format is designed to make the trading process visible before results are judged.

Competition is used as a format for learning: members compare process, review decisions, and strengthen their understanding of the Gurman Method.

Competition process

Register

Join the competition, confirm the format, and review the rules before the round starts.

Trade Under Rules

Execute decisions through the Wheel Strategy framework while following position sizing limits and risk guidelines.

Climb The Leaderboard

Track your performance in real time and compete based on consistency, discipline, and execution quality.

Scoring Criteria

01 / Process Score

Rule compliance, setup quality, journaling, and trade review discipline.

02 / Risk Score

Position sizing, exposure control, drawdown awareness, and defined-risk planning.

03 / Consistency Score

Repeatable decision-making and improvement across the full challenge window.
