frequently asked questions

Common questions about Oscar's method

view all FAQs

What is the Gurman Method?

It is Oscar's repeatable process for reading market context, selecting Wheel Strategy trades, managing risk, and reviewing decisions.

Is Oscar teaching signals or a system?

The focus is the system. Trade ideas can be discussed, but the priority is understanding the logic behind the decision.

Does the method focus only on the Wheel Strategy?

The Wheel Strategy is the core application, but the method also teaches market context, risk control, and review discipline.

Can beginners follow the course?

Yes. The course starts with options foundations and builds toward trade selection, execution, and review.

Why does Oscar emphasize process so much?

Because discipline is what makes a trader's decisions repeatable. Returns are reviewed after the process is clear.

Where should I start?

Start with the course if you need the foundation. Join the membership if you want to see the method applied weekly.
