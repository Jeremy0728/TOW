Common questions before you join

view all FAQs

What is the Gurman Method?

It is Oscar's repeatable process for reading market context, selecting Wheel Strategy trades, managing risk, and reviewing decisions.

What exactly do I get with VIP?

VIP gives you community access, live trade alerts, weekly Q&A, strategy breakdowns, and ongoing education around the Gurman Method.

Do I need experience to start?

No. Beginners can start with the basics, while experienced traders can use the community to sharpen execution and risk management.

What is your approach to risk management?

Risk comes first. We teach defined risk, position sizing, trade planning, and when to stay out instead of forcing trades.

Is this financial advice?

No. Trading On Wheels is trading education and community. We share methods, analysis and our own trades, not personalized financial advice.

Where should I start?

Start with the course if you need the foundation. Join the membership if you want to see the method applied weekly.
