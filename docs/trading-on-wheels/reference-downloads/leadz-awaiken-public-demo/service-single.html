Ready to become a Gurman Wheeler?

join now

The Trading On Wheels course teaches the Gurman Method through a practical sequence: market structure, options foundations, Wheel Strategy execution, scoring and trade review.

The goal is not random trades. The course is built to help you create a disciplined routine, understand risk before entry and repeat the process with clarity.

  • step-by-step training
  • beginner to wheeler
  • wheel strategy execution

What you will learn

The course is organized around the same sequence Oscar uses to evaluate trades: understand the market, filter the setup, define the risk, execute with rules, and review the decision after the trade.

01

options foundations

Understand puts, calls, premium, assignment, expiration, and the mechanics behind the Wheel Strategy.

02

wheel strategy execution

Learn when to sell puts, how to manage assignment, and how covered calls fit into a repeatable process.

03

trade selection logic

Use market context, sectors, liquidity, strike selection, and scoring before considering any setup.

04

risk and review

Build rules for position sizing, portfolio exposure, exits, adjustments, and post-trade learning.

Course structure

The curriculum is designed to be studied in order, then revisited while reviewing real trade cards and weekly market context inside the TOW ecosystem.

  • Options foundations and terminology
  • Wheel Strategy rules and examples
  • Gurman Method scoring framework
  • Trade card review process
  • Risk management and sizing rules
  • Repeatable weekly routine

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Do I need options experience before joining?

No. The course starts with options foundations, then moves into the Wheel Strategy and the Gurman Method step by step.

Is this course about trade alerts?

No. The course is built around logic, process, and decision-making so you understand why a trade may or may not fit the rules.

How does the membership connect to the course?

The course teaches the framework. The membership shows how the framework is applied around live market context and weekly reviews.

Can I use the course with a demo account?

Yes. Practicing in a demo environment is encouraged before putting real capital at risk.
